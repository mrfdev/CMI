You can type:{#gold} /%player_world% &l<command>
And the {#gold}<command>&r part can be: &olevel, count, help, generator, team, border, menu, challenges&r, and more. Enjoy.

{#gold}help&r - will give you additional help, can also be used per command.
{#gold}level&r - will recalculate your island level, which is handy for the generator and challenges.
{#gold}challenges&r - a great way to complete challenges on your island to get extra items.
{#gold}generator&r - upgrade your standard cobblestone generator to get more out of it.
{#gold}border&r - a way to toggle the visualization of the island border.
{#gold}team&r - lets you manage your team setup so you can play with friends. Read /teams for more information.

On OneBlock, you can also use{#gold} phases&r and{#gold} count&r.
